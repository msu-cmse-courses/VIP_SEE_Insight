## The VIP Model

The Vertically Integrated Projects (VIP) Program is a nonprofit alliance of colleges and universities that engages students in long-term, large-scale, faculty-led research. 




## The VIP Consortium

Currently, VIP includes 50+ universities across 13 countries, each adapting the model in its own way to fit their institutional culture. Students from all backgrounds from STEM to Humanities are encouraged to participate.
